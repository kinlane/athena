Frustration with patent trolls, and momentum for reform, has been building for some time now.Today, the stakes got even higher when the White House announced that it was actively taking on the troll problem.This is big news, and not just because of the seven legislative proposals the White House recommends (more on those below).Even more important are the five executive actions the President intends to take with or without congressional help.The news here is good.
